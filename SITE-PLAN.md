# Hole 19 - Site Plan

## Business Overview

Hole 19 is a local golf shop in Springfield, Illinois specializing in golf equipment and custome fitting services. 

**Target audience:**  Amateur to Professional (25-50) golf players who want to improve their game by purchasing all the equipment they need and have clubs that fit their swing and skill.

**Brand personality:** Helpful, knowledgable, and reliable. A place where everyone feels welcomed and will be helped out with any questions and reasonable requests with quality and care.
