About Hole 19 Golf Shop

Family-owned and operated since 2008

Our Story

Hole 19 Golf Shop began in 2008 when founder David Mitchell turned his lifelong passion for golf into something more. After 20 years as a PGA club professional, David saw an opportunity to create a different kind of golf retail experience in Springfield.

What started as a small shop has grown into a 4,500 square foot facility with three fitting studios and a full repair workshop.
